What is ReviveOR?

  • Trade-In

    Purchasing an Ocean Republic jewellery piece is part of a process. After you have enjoyed your jewellery piece for some time, you may want to buy something new. With ReviveOR, you can trade your piece for credit. This credit can be used to purchase a new jewellery piece on the Ocean Republic website.

  • Shop Used

    ReviveOR sells previously owned jewellery pieces in the store. All of these pieces have been checked thoroughly and have been refurbished where necessary. Each piece has a unique story that you could be a part of. Visit the shop to find something to your liking.

  • Stories

    Every piece of jewellery has a unique story. They are with you through the good and bad times. Every location you've been, every person you've met, your jewellery piece is right there with you. ReviveOR allows people to share these stories. Have a look!

How does it work?

The trade-in process can be started by going to the Trade-In page. From there you will have to identify your jewellery piece for us. You can also tell us the condition your jewellery piece is in. Based on what you tell us, you will instantly receive an indication of how much credit you could expect to receive for your jewellery piece. You can then decide if you wish to trade your piece in for this amount.
